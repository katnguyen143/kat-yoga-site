welcome

hi, i'm kat!

I teach yoga and acroyoga at Nestra Beach Shala in Baan Tai, Koh Phangan. I teach a variety of yoga classes, ranging from calming yang to yin sunset flows to more energizing handstand and inversion flows. My classes are a space to breathe deeply, move mindfully, and reconnect with your body with the sound of the waves or from the comfort of your home.

Each session is a chance to move with the breeze, shake off the tension, and feel a little more spacious in your body and mind.

what I offer

yoga and acroyoga classes

group yoga classes

Flow with the ocean breeze at Nestra Beach Shala: energizing vinyasa mornings, gentle yang to yin sunset flows, and playful handstand and inversions.

private yoga classes

Take time for yourself with a personalized yoga practice designed around your body, energy, and goals. Whether you're looking to build strength, release tension, or simply slow down and breathe, we’ll move at your pace and tune into what you need most. Sessions are held in-person at Nestra Beach Shala or online from wherever you are.

private acro yoga sessions

Learn the art of lifting with confidence. As a seasoned flyer, I specialize in teaching bases how to support, balance, and communicate clearly in AcroYoga. Whether you're just starting out or refining your technique, we’ll build trust, strength, and playful flow at your own pace.
