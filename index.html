Welcome

Hi, I’m Kathryn.

I teach yoga and acroyoga at the beautiful Nestra Beach Shala in Koh Phangan. My classes are a space to breathe deeply, move mindfully, and reconnect with your body — with the sound of the waves or from the comfort of home.

Every session invites you to explore strength, softness, and playfulness, so you leave feeling lighter, calmer, and more alive.
